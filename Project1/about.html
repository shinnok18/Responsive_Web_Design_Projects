About Us

The foundations of Aydınlı Group were laid in 1965 with the opening of the first store in Istanbul Fatih, under the name of "Aydınlı Yerli Mallar". The beginning of multi-storey retailing in the 1970s, the wholesale of men's and women's products under the Aydınlı brand, the establishment of the first workshop and reaching 200 dealers. In 1989, AYDINLI HAZIR GİYİM SANAYİ VE TİCARET A.Ş. is established and the production facilities equipped with the latest technological possibilities of the day begin their activities in 1990. The 90s are an important turning point in the history of the group with the international agreements signed. Pierre Cardin in 1993, Cacharel in 1995 and U.S. Polo Assn. Growth gains momentum with the contracts signed with. With its more than 4,000 employees, it is one of the largest ready-to-wear retailers not only in Turkey but also in its immediate geography. The Aydınlı Group, which adopts the principle of quality service, pays attention to universal values, produces environmentally conscious and is structured at world standards with its customer-oriented working system, is one of the leading companies in the ready-to-wear sector. It carried its success to the construction sector and broke new ground in Turkey with the Solarkent project, which generates electricity from solar energy. Aydınlı Group, which reinforces its presence in this sector with the Koru Florya project and brings a different perspective to the housing sector with Vadi İstanbul; continues to provide added value to the country's economy with its investments and new projects in the textile, ready-made clothing retailing and construction sectors. Offering functionality and diversity with its collections prepared for gentleman and stylish men, who have a noticeable elegance in all areas of life, who are interested in healthy life and sports, Pierre Cardin combines quality and comfort with the power and mastery of design, confident and socializing with a noticeable elegance in any environment. Cacharel, who joined the group in 1995, dates back to 1962. It is considered a symbol of elegance, modernism and the art of elegant dressing in the world. Cacharel has adopted a simple but high quality and differentiated appearance in design, which supports its stylish stance with functional details. The brand is the indispensable choice of men who know what they want, are free, travellers, love to explore, but at the same time sophistication and give importance to design and quality hidden in details. Polo Assn. is the representative of polo sport in America and Canada. The brand, which was born from the sport of polo, which is also known as the sport of kings and nobles in history, carries the inspiration from this sport and the lifestyle it offers to its collections. Classic U.S. Polo Assn. While the colors and coat of arms support this style, it also reflects the spirit of the sport. With its unique, comfortable and stylish models, the U.S. Polo Assn. is the favorite brand of both young people and families.
